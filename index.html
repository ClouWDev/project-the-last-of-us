The Last of Us is a post-apocalyptic American television series that premiered on January 15, 2023 via HBO. Based on the 2013 video game of the same name developed by Naughty Dog, the series follows Joel (Pedro Pascal), a smuggler tasked with escorting teenage Ellie (Bella Ramsey) across a post-apocalyptic United States. It also features Tommy (Gabriel Luna), Joel's younger brother and former soldier.
